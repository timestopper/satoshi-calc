What is a Satoshi?

A satoshi is the smallest unit of the Bitcoin cryptocurrency recorded on the blockchain.

It is a one hundred millionth of a single bitcoin (0.00000001 BTC). The unit was named in homage to the original creator of Bitcoin, Satoshi Nakamoto.

  • 0.00000001  1 satoshi
  • 0.00000010  10 satoshi
  • 0.00000100  100 satoshi - 1 Bit / μBTC (you-bit)
  • 0.00001000  1,000 satoshi
  • 0.00010000  10,000 satoshi
  • 0.00100000  100,000 satoshi - 1 mBTC (em-bit)
  • 0.01000000  1,000,000 satoshi - 1 cBTC (bitcent)
  • 0.10000000  10,000,000 satoshi
  • 1.00000000  100,000,000 satoshi - 1 Bitcoin - BTC
